Chapter 1. Introduction

Whiteboard is an online tool to facilitate TA office hours and extra help sessions. It allows TAs to create a virtual "classroom" to which students can join. This eliminates the need for going to a classroom, and also provides extra motivation for joining TA office hours due to the fact that no real effort is involved.

This application is centered around the server/client relationship. The TA is the server, and the clients connect to him. The TA also exhibits authority over the students in the form of admin commands and restritced access to certain program functions.

Whiteboard features:

  • Basic IM-style chat.

  • A virtual shared whiteboard that the TA can draw on.

  • A list of all the people currently in the session, not counting the admin.

  • A status window which displays what is going on behind the scenes.

  • Special operations for fun, such as /me and /roll (discussed in Client Usage).

  • Admin operations such as /kick and /ban.
